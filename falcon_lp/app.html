Workflows

Operational Workflows

Follow the journey of a flight through the system — from pre-flight preparation to post-flight reporting.

A

Pre-flight Preparation

Before operations begin, confirm all gates are assigned, staff is scheduled, and passenger services are ready. Verify that all required documents and clearances are in order.

Gate Assignment Staff Check Document Verification
B

Active Operations

Monitor flight status updates in real-time. When delays or cancellations occur, quickly notify affected teams, adjust gate assignments, and update passenger information systems.

Real-time Monitoring Alert Management Gate Reassignment
C

Post-flight Processing

Record flight outcomes, log any incidents or delays, and generate reports for stakeholders. Export data for analysis and ensure all audit trails are complete.

Outcome Logging Report Generation Data Export
